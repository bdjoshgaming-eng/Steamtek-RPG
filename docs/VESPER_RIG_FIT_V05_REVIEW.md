# Vesper Kane Rig-Fit v0.5 Review

## Purpose

This revision corrects the overly thin side silhouette seen in the v0.4 runtime review while preserving Vesper's established height, front silhouette, rig, animations, mechanical-arm side, and +40-degree facing correction.

## Changes from v0.4

- Increased front-to-back depth on the coat torso, coat tails, shoulder cape, head, hat, and boots.
- Added side coat gussets so the torso reads as a garment instead of a flat panel.
- Added a compact rear pressure regulator with two pressure cells and a cyan status indicator.
- Added front placket and belt depth for stronger three-quarter readability.
- Preserved the physical-left mechanical arm.

## Runtime review

Open `res://scenes/tests/hybrid_3d/VesperKane_RigFit_v05.tscn` and run the current scene with **F6**.

Check:

- front, rear, and both side profiles;
- idle and walking playback;
- straight-down facing with the permanent +40-degree correction;
- obstacle occlusion and contact shadows;
- mechanical arm remains on Vesper's physical left.

## Acceptance target

Vesper should no longer appear paper-thin from the side. The silhouette should read as a layered coat, body, boots, and compact pressure apparatus without becoming bulky or changing her overall height.
