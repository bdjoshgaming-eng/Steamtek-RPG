KEY SYSTEMS (current state)
---------------------------------------------------------------
XP SYSTEM
- Two base pools only: "Combat XP" and "Crafting XP". No Thug Points.
- _add_skill_xp(pool, amount) applies XP, clamped to _get_xp_type_cap().
- _resolve_xp_pool(source) maps a logical activity to its pool. This is
  the SINGLE place to branch XP by specialization later.
    Healing    -> Combat XP   (pre-specialization)
    Scavenging -> Crafting XP
- Crafting, scanning, sampling all award Crafting XP.
- Kills award Combat XP (split by weapon-class damage share).

KEYSTONE / TALENT SYSTEM (Street Thug)
- Four keystones: Melee, Ranged, Crafting, Auxiliary.
- Melee/Ranged: ability triangle in the center (no hex), stat ring outside.
- Crafting/Auxiliary: hex + stat ring only, no abilities.
- KeystoneViewer.gd renders the whole panel in code.
- UNLOCK all 4 keystones -> Enforcer/Specialist advancement paths light up.
- MASTER all 4 (every point spent) -> Street Thug diamond turns gold
  ("MASTERED" state). These are two different milestones.
