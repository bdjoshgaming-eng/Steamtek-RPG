# Vesper Kane Rig-Fit v0.4 Review

## Purpose

V0.4 is the first Vesper identity-detail pass built on the validated v0.3 gameplay-readable silhouette.

## Identity additions

- More visibly asymmetrical coat construction
- Extended scarf and rear hair silhouette
- Brass monocle frame, bridge clip, and chain
- Mechanical-arm elbow ring, pressure hose, gauge bezel, and shoulder pressure plate
- Compact pneumatic sidearm and holster on the non-mechanical side
- Living-hand glove and knuckle protection
- Neutral chest badge with restrained cyan diagnostic tick
- Persistent mechanical arm remains on physical left
- Exported mesh count increased from 109 to 135
