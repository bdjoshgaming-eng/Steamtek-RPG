IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS

This is a combat-animation carrier and Godot integration prototype, not a
finished Hazmat NPC.

- The visible mesh is the approved base body, not Hazmat clothing.
- No actual rifle scene is assigned yet.
- The rifle mount follows RightHand, but final rifle position and rotation
  require tuning against the selected rifle model.
- No muzzle marker, muzzle flash, projectile spawn, or firing audio is included.
- No enemy AI, navigation, targeting, perception, health, damage, cover logic,
  combat stats, or loot behavior is included.
- No final Hazmat materials, armor, mask, tank, hoses, or other outfit parts
  are included.

Do not mislabel the current visible proxy as the finished Hazmat art.
