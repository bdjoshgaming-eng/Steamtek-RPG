# Vesper Kane Rig-Fit v0.1 Review

## Purpose

This is the first character-art pass after the Steamtek hybrid 3D proof was accepted. It is a silhouette and rig-fit checkpoint, not the final sculpt or texture pass.

## Preserved technical contract

- Approved `Steamtek_HumanRig_v1` armature
- `STK_IDLE` and `STK_WALK` animation names
- 706-bone source rig
- 230 imported animation tracks per clip in Godot
- Existing world scale and boot contact
- Existing hybrid movement controller
- Locked orthographic 60-degree azimuth / 30-degree elevation camera
- Measured +40-degree C002 visual-forward correction retained for the review scene

## Vesper silhouette included

- Tall top hat
- High collar
- Long split coat tails
- Black/gunmetal material blockout
- Controlled aged-brass construction
- Cyan monocle and restrained technology accents
- Persistent mechanical arm on physical left
- Asymmetrical right-hip equipment

## Intentionally deferred

- Final face and hair sculpt
- Cloth folds and weathering
- Production PBR textures
- Coat-tail secondary physics
- Mechanical-hand articulation detail
- Raven drone attachment
- Final in-game scale polish
