# Steamtek Lighting Fixture Detail References

Status: approved secondary micro-design references

These crops guide the small-scale construction language of reusable lighting
fixtures, control panels, and environmental indicators. They supplement the
approved full-scene Steamtek references and do not replace the holistic scene,
palette, material, wet-surface, or architectural direction.

## Production interpretation

- Preserve dark neo-industrial structure as the dominant read.
- Use cyan and magenta as compact luminous cores, not full-object colors.
- Favor recessed lenses, thick frames, bolts, chamfers, and protected housings.
- Pair small amber indicators with cyan or magenta only when functionally clear.
- Keep fixtures modular: root, mount plane, light origin, and final mesh remain
  separable.
- Continue supporting both building axes through root rotation only.
