# Vesper Kane Rig-Fit v0.6 Review

## Purpose

V0.6 is the first deformation-focused Vesper revision. It preserves the approved v0.5 silhouette and replaces the rigid single-bone coat-tail setup with controlled hip-to-leg weighting.

## Changes from v0.5

- Reweighted the two front coat tails from the hips into their neighboring thighs.
- Reweighted the rear tail across the hips and both thighs.
- Reweighted the side tails with the same controlled gradient.
- Added an overlapping waist underlay so the coat does not open into an empty gap while walking.
- Added small hip seam covers and a flexible waist band.
- Preserved character height, ground contact, materials, mechanical-arm side, rig, and animation clips.

## Runtime review

Open `res://scenes/tests/hybrid_3d/VesperKane_RigFit_v06.tscn` and press **F6**.

Review:

- coat continuity while walking forward and diagonally;
- front and rear leg clearance;
- both side profiles;
- idle-to-walk blending;
- foot planting and ground contact;
- physical-left mechanical arm consistency.

## Scope

This is still a rig-fit production model, not the final texture/sculpt pass. Final surface detail, material wear, garment refinement, and bespoke personality animation come after the deformation foundation is accepted.
